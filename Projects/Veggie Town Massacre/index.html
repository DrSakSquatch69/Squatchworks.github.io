Veggie Town Massacre Gameplay

About

Veggie Town Massacre is a chaotic Unity-based team project developed with Team "Algorithm Architects". Players defend against increasingly difficult waves of mutated vegetable foes using a variety of weapons and power-ups.

The game focuses on fast-paced wave survival gameplay with escalating enemy difficulty. I owned the AI systems, level design, and overall UI architecture to ensure a smooth and engaging player experience.

My Contributions

  • Designed and implemented the enemy AI systems including movement patterns, targeting logic, and wave-based difficulty scaling
  • Built the UI architecture — HUD, health bars, wave indicators, and game state screens
  • Created and iterated on the level design to support varied enemy spawn configurations
  • Implemented the wave manager for spawning, pacing, and progression across difficulty tiers
  • Collaborated with the team on player mechanics integration and polish across development sprints

Features

  • Wave System — Progressive enemy wave spawning with increasing difficulty and variety per wave
  • Enemy AI — Pathfinding and targeting logic for vegetable enemy types
  • Player Combat — Multiple weapons and power-ups for surviving the assault
  • UI Architecture — Full HUD with health, ammo, wave counter, and score tracking
  • Level Design — Custom map layout designed to complement AI spawn and player movement
  • Team Project — Built collaboratively with Team "Algorithm Architects" using Unity
