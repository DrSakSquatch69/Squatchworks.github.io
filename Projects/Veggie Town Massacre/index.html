Veggie Town Massacre Unity Wave Shooter

A chaotic Unity-based team project where players defend against waves of mutated vegetables.

Project Overview

Veggie Town Massacre is a collaborative project developed with Team "Algorithm Architects". The game challenges players to survive increasingly difficult waves of vegetable foes using a variety of weapons and power-ups.

In this project, I owned the AI systems, level design, and the overall UI architecture, ensuring a smooth and engaging player experience.
