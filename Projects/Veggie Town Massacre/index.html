Veggie Town Massacre

Unity C# Wave Shooter AI Systems Team Project

About

Veggie Town Massacre is a chaotic Unity-based team project developed with Team "Algorithm Architects". Players defend against increasingly difficult waves of mutated vegetable foes using a variety of weapons and power-ups.

The game focuses on fast-paced wave survival gameplay with escalating enemy difficulty. I owned the AI systems, level design, and overall UI architecture to ensure a smooth and engaging player experience.

My Contributions

  • Designed and implemented the enemy AI system including patrol, chase, and attack behaviors using Unity NavMesh
  • Built the wave spawning system with configurable difficulty scaling and enemy type weighting per wave
  • Designed and iterated on the game level including enemy spawn zones and player navigation paths
  • Developed the in-game UI architecture including health bar, wave counter, and score display
  • Collaborated with teammates on weapon systems and integrated AI responses to player actions

Features

  • Wave Survival — Increasingly difficult waves of mutated vegetable enemies with configurable scaling
  • Enemy AI — NavMesh-driven patrol, chase, and attack behaviors
  • Weapon System — Multiple weapon types with varied damage and fire rate
  • Power-ups — Collectible power-ups that modify player stats and abilities
  • Level Design — Purposefully designed arena with spawn zones and cover
  • UI System — Live health, wave progress, and score tracking
