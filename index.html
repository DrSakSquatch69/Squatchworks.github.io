About Me

I am a highly driven Gameplay Programmer specializing in **C++ engine architecture** and **Unreal Engine 5** development. My expertise lies in the bridge between technical design and gameplay systems—whether it's building custom Vulkan renderers from the ground up or architecting complex AI behavior trees in Unity.

Currently nearing completion of my Bachelor's degree in Game Development at Full Sail University, I have spent the last few years mastering the systems that make games work. From handling low-level memory management to deploying global leaderboard APIs, I focus on creating code that is as scalable as it is performant.

Beyond the IDE, I'm a single father to two boys and a passionate follower of futures markets. I bring a "real-world" work ethic—forged through a decade of experience in high-pressure manufacturing and management roles—to every development sprint I join.
